Din Tai Fung
鼎泰豐 — Da'an, Taipei
Order: Pork XLB (NT$105/5), truffle XLB (NT$450/5), chicken soup. Cash only. Go before 11:30am or after 2pm to skip the worst lines. Dongmen MRT Exit 5.
Michelin Map →
Yongkang Beef Noodle
永康牛肉麵 — Da'an, Taipei
Order: Braised beef in spicy broth (NT$230). Add steamed spare ribs and large intestines. Bib Gourmand. 11am-8:30pm. Cash only. Dongmen MRT, 10 min walk.
Bib Gourmand Map →
Fuhang Soy Milk
阜杭豆漿 — Zhongzheng, Taipei
Order: Thick shaobing, salty soy milk with vinegar, dan bing. Opens 5:30am, closed Mon. Weekday before 7am = 20 min wait. Weekend = 1hr+. Know your order before you reach the counter. Cash only.
Breakfast Map →
Jin Feng Lu Rou Fan
金峰魯肉飯 — Zhongzheng, Taipei
Order: Large braised pork rice (NT$50), stewed egg (NT$15), pork rib soup. Open 8am-1am. Total meal ~NT$100 ($3). CKS Memorial MRT Exit 2, 50m walk. Cash only.
Street food Map →
Fuzhou Pepper Pork Bun
福州世祖胡椒餅 — Raohe, Taipei
Charcoal-baked in a clay oven. Crispy shell, juicy pepper pork and scallion filling. ~NT$60 each. Right at Raohe market entrance. Always a 20-30 min queue. Worth it.
Night market Map →
A-Mei Tea House
阿妹茶樓 — Jiufen
Tea + sweets set NT$300. Oolong in a 100-year-old building with ocean views and red lanterns. Go at dusk for magic lighting. Weekends get packed. The teahouses across the street are less crowded with similar views.
Tea Map →
Chun Shui Tang
春水堂 — Taichung
Order: Original Pearl Milk Tea (NT$70-140). Invented here in 1983. Go mid-afternoon to skip crowds. 20 min walk from Taichung TRA station. Basement area is cozier. No photos at the counter.
Tea / Origin Map →
Du Xiao Yue
度小月 — Tainan
Order: Dan zai noodles — same recipe since 1895. Small bowl, shrimp-pork broth, minced meat. Tainan's most famous single dish. Sit at the counter to watch them cook.
Since 1895 Map →
Addiction Aquatic Development
上引水產 — Zhongshan, Taipei
Luxury seafood market + sushi bar + grill. Sashimi platters, standing sushi counter with Japanese chef, live seafood tanks. 7am-10pm. Cash only (!). Eat at Rongxing Park nearby.
Seafood market Map →
Ningxia Night Market
寧夏夜市 — Datong, Taipei
Compact, food-focused, beloved by locals. Best lu rou fan in the city. Taro balls, oyster omelets, no tourist crush. Open every night. Near Dihua Street — combine the two.
Night market Map →
Miaokou Night Market
廟口夜市 — Keelung
Taiwan's best seafood night market. Stalls numbered for decades. Crab thick soup, ding bian cuo, tempura, peanut ice cream rolls. Steps from the harbor — it doesn't get fresher.
Night market Map →
Shilin Night Market
士林夜市 — Shilin, Taipei
Taiwan's biggest and most famous night market. Underground food court for giant fried chicken, stinky tofu, oyster omelets. Touristy but still fun. Jiantan MRT.
Night market Map →
A Hsing Milkfish Congee
阿星鹹粥 — Tainan
Bib Gourmand since 1965. Queue starts at 5am. Ask for belly cut — fattiest, silkiest part of the milkfish. Congee with fried garlic on top. Air-conditioned inside. THE Tainan milkfish experience.
Bib Gourmand Map →
Ice Monster
ICE MONSTER — Da'an, Taipei
Pioneered Taiwan's shaved ice revolution. Towering bowls of mango shaved ice made from frozen fruit blocks, not plain ice. Best in summer (mango season May-Oct).
Dessert Map →
Hayashi Department Store
林百貨 — Tainan
Restored 1932 Art Deco building with rooftop cafe. Local crafts, Tainan treats, and WWII bullet scars on the walls. A beautiful spot for a break and souvenirs.

Beautiful Places

Taiwan's scenery is wildly underrated
Tap the places you'd want to see. These are the "I can't believe Taiwan looks like this" moments.
Taroko Gorge
Marble-walled canyons plunge hundreds of meters while turquoise rivers vanish into the mountain's throat
Qingshui Cliffs
1,000m sheer drops into three shades of blue Pacific — East Asia's most dramatic coast
Alishan Sunrise
Board a century-old railway into the clouds. Golden light breaks over a sea of fog above 2,000m peaks
Sun Moon Lake at Dawn
Mirror-still water in a volcanic basin where morning mist erases the treeline
Jiufen at Dusk
Red lanterns cascade down stone stairways into the mist, tea and taro drifting from doorways above the Pacific
Yehliu Geopark
Wind and wave sculpted a peninsula of sandstone into surreal mushroom rocks and the famous Queen's Head
Gaomei Wetlands
Infinite sunset reflections on tidal flats — walk the boardwalk as the sky turns liquid gold
Xiao Liuqiu
Crystal water, coral island, and sea turtles swimming right beside you — Taiwan's tropical secret
Elephant Mountain
20-minute climb through jungle to Taipei 101 glowing against the city skyline. Best at sunset.
East Coast Highway 11
Cliffs, surf breaks, rice paddies meeting the ocean — Taiwan's most beautiful drive
Beitou Hot Springs
Sulfur-scented valley 30 min from downtown Taipei. Japanese-era bathhouses line a steaming creek
Yangmingshan
Steaming volcanic vents, calla lily fields in spring, and misty trails above Taipei
Alishan sunrise requires a 5am train. Gaomei Wetlands are magical only at sunset. Jiufen lanterns glow at dusk. Are you willing to plan around the light?
